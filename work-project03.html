The Client

Jellyvision

Jellyvision creates interactive software, like ALEX, to simplify complex information, helping employees make informed decisions about benefits through engaging and user-friendly virtual counseling.

The Project

At Jellyvision, I undertook a project to create numerous images for the company's blog, which required each image to encapsulate the concept of its corresponding blog post. The complexity of visualizing these concepts varied, with some being straightforward and others more challenging to represent. Collaborating closely with the marketing department, we brainstormed various ideas and concepts until we arrived at the perfect visual representation for each blog entry.

All the images adhered to Jellyvision's brand style at the time, which featured a simple paper cut-out aesthetic. This style involved creating solid vector images with a slight drop shadow at the edges, giving the illusion that the paper was floating on the page. This approach not only ensured brand consistency but also contributed to a visually appealing and fun style that made the project both enjoyable and creatively stimulating.
