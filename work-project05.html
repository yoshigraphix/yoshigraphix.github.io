Coding Fun, One Card at a Time

UI/UX · Tynker

Crafted over 100 visually engaging thumbnail cards for Tynker’s web and mobile app to attract and engage users.

The Client

Tynker

Tynker is an innovative platform that teaches kids coding and computer programming through fun and interactive lessons. Their web and mobile app offer a wide range of courses and projects designed to make learning to code accessible and engaging for children of all ages.

The Project

I was tasked with designing over 100 thumbnail cards for Tynker’s web and mobile app. These thumbnail cards serve as visual entry points to various lessons, each designed to be both informative and enticing. The goal was to create thumbnails that would capture the essence of each lesson and motivate users to click and engage with the content.

Each thumbnail card features a centered title with images and objects that reflect the lesson’s theme. The design process involved balancing aesthetics with clarity, ensuring that each card was visually appealing while accurately representing the lesson content. This project allowed me to combine creativity with strategic design to enhance user engagement on Tynker's platform.
