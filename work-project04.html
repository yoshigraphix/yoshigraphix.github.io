The Client

Jackbos Games & Tynker

Jackbox Games is a leading game studio known for the "Jackbox Party Pack" series, offering fun, humorous party games played with smartphones as controllers. Tynker is an educational tech company that teaches kids coding through interactive, fun courses, fostering early interest in computer science and STEM.

The Project

In my role at Tynker and Jackbox Games, I spearheaded the creation of marketing images that played a pivotal role in driving traffic and attracting customers to the platforms. With Tynker, I crafted visually engaging graphics that showcased the platform's educational programming offerings, including courses in game design, web design, animation, and robotics. These images served as compelling visual representations of Tynker's mission to help K-12 learners acquire coding skills in a fun and interactive manner.

Similarly, at Jackbox Games, I leveraged my creativity to develop marketing images that captured the essence of the games and appealed to the target audience. By infusing humor and wit into the visuals, I succeeded in creating content that not only entertained but also encouraged user engagement. These images played a vital role in attracting new customers and retaining existing ones, contributing to the overall success of the platform. Through strategic placement across various digital channels, including social media, email, and banners, these marketing images effectively communicated the unique value propositions of Tynker and Jackbox Games, ultimately driving traffic and fostering customer acquisition.
