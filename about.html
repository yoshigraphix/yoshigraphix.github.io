About Me

I'm Yoshi Sakaguchi, an Artistic Imagineer based in East Tennessee, United States.

I'm passionate about creating engaging and easy-to-navigate layouts. I love the look of clean lines, sharp contrasts in color schemes, or bold graphic assets paired with sophisticated illustrations.

As a multimedia artist, my work is centered around crafting dynamic visual experiences. I specialize in designing immersive environments and captivating graphics that resonate with audiences across various platforms.
